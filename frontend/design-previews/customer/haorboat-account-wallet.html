Where credit comes from

the invoice only ever moves down

🪑 Someone joins your open seat

You booked a room solo at the buyout price. When another party takes the spare place, your invoice drops by their share and commission is recalculated. If you already paid more than the new total, the surplus becomes customer_credit.

🔁 A cheaper reschedule

When you move a trip to a quieter, lower-priced date, your carried-over advance can exceed the new total. The difference lands here as credit instead of a refund.
